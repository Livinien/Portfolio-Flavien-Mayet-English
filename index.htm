About

My name is Flavien MAYET, I started in the professional world working in the audiovisual sector as a cameraman and video editor for web TV at the University of Limoges.



I worked during July-August 2016 as a multimedia salesperson at Boulanger, a multimedia and household appliances store. My job was to alternate between customer relations and taking care of the smartphone and laptop sections.




Between 2018 and 2019, I left for the United States to improve my English language and discover another culture in order to return to France with several strings to my bow.




When I returned to France, I wanted to orient myself in everything related to digital and new technologies.
This is where I discovered the profession of WEB DEVELOPER.

I started to be interested in this profession and then I started training on Elephorm then on Udemy, starting with basic languages like HTML/CSS, then came later JavaScript.




I subsequently decided to complete online training to become a web developer by myself, called OpenClassrooms. During this training, I continued to delve deeper into the basic languages and learn the back-end side.


I then completed another training course as an APPLICATION DESIGNER DEVELOPER with the Simplon school in Grenoble.



This is why today, I am actively looking for a company to put into practice my knowledge acquired during my two training courses and thus improve my skills.
